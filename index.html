STEWARDSHIP PROPERTY MANAGEMENT

Our mission is to help each client achieve their investment goals while showing each and every resident the respect and responsiveness they deserve. We believe in the good ole-fashioned "Golden Rule" and use it as the standard in every aspect of our business.

WHY HIRE US VS. SELF-MANAGING?

Significantly Less Stress & Time Involvement

One of the single greatest struggles of owning rental real estate can
be the day to day involvement of responding to residents,
coordinating vacancy renovations/repairs, showing vacant units,
chasing delinquent rents, and on and on!... This is one of the key
reasons why investors reach out to us again and again.

We're an Active Investor-Led Company

Unlike most management companies, our owner is an active investor
and Stewardship client himself. Stewardship was born from a need to
take a different and truly strategic approach to handling every aspect
of property management, material sourcing, and the resident
relationship.

Better Screening Process, Better Residents

Because of our screening software/tools & extensive resources, we
can screen more applicants, and with more filters. At times, we even
have an applicant waiting list for properties due to multiple marketing
sources, many different locations (often) available simultaneously,
and momentum from being in the business for nearly 10 years.

Risk Mitigation

Our team goes through consistent and ongoing fair housing training
to ensure we treat each person we interact with as an equal in every
way. We also periodically invite staff from nearby fair housing offices
to visit and keep us aware of any policy changes, etc. At Stewardship.
we don't just treat everyone equally because it's the law, we do it
because it's the right thing to do!
